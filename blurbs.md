# Book Blurbs

One paragraph per book. The ID on each `##` line must match the book's `id` in `registry.json`.
`build_catalog.py` reads this file; changes here take effect on the next `python3 build_catalog.py` run.

---

## artofgig-vol1 — Foundations (Art of Gig, Vol. 1)

Thirty-two essays on the basics of surviving and thriving as an independent: getting oriented, bootstrapping a practice, and managing the consulting psyche.

## artofgig-vol2 — Superstructures (Art of Gig, Vol. 2)

Thirty-four essays on the wider landscape of free agency: the history and future of the gig economy, creative frames for client work, and the long haul.

## artofgig-vol3 — The Yakverse Chronicles (Art of Gig, Vol. 3)

Thirteen absurdist consulting tales — yaks, gigsters, and capricious clients in increasingly surreal engagements.

## twitterbook — vgr: The Twitter Years (2007–22)

102 chapters: a curated compendium of 396 singles plus 101 threads — the complete Twitter writings of Venkatesh Rao (@vgr).

## breakingsmart — Breaking Smart

21 essays on the software revolution — how agile, networked approaches are disrupting industries and remaking society. In English, German, and French.

## mediocratopia — Mediocratopia

13 essays on mediocrity as aspiration — optimization resistance, withheld reserves, and the courage to slouch toward a mediocracy utopia.

## gervais-principle — The Gervais Principle

The definitive Ribbonfarm series on organizational life through the lens of The Office — sociopaths, clueless middle management, and losers navigating institutional reality.

## be-slightly-evil — Be Slightly Evil

A Machiavellian guide to organizational life — essays on power, leverage, and strategic imperfection for those who live and work in institutions.

## crash-early-crash-often — Crash Early, Crash Often

Collected Ribbonfarm essays on failure, resilience, and the counterintuitive wisdom of embracing setbacks as a navigational tool.
